Our Services

Purified Water Vending Stations

Our automated water vending stations provide 24/7 access to clean, purified drinking water. Using reverse osmosis and UV purification technology, we ensure that every drop of water meets the highest safety standards.

With convenient locations throughout the community and affordable pricing, we make clean water accessible to everyone.

  • Advanced purification process
  • Affordable pricing
  • Convenient locations
  • 24/7 access

Poshomill Services

Our modern poshomill services allow customers to mill their grains into fine flour while preserving nutritional value. We offer different milling options to suit various needs.

Whether you need flour for ugali, porridge, or baking, we can mill your grains to the perfect consistency.

  • Modern milling technology
  • Multiple grinding options
  • Nutrition preservation
  • Affordable rates

Cereal Wholesale & Retail

We source high-quality cereals directly from farmers and reputable suppliers. Our products include maize, wheat, millet, sorghum, and beans available in both wholesale and retail quantities.

We ensure proper storage and handling to maintain quality and prevent contamination.

  • High-quality grains
  • Competitive pricing
  • Bulk and retail options
  • Proper storage facilities
